And with that, the year 2025 has almost come to a close. And so has my computer science studies apparently. I am currently enrolled in a double degree of science and computer science which commenced in 2022, so even though I have finished all of the requirements for my computer science side of my degree, I still have a semester's worth of science courses left to go. However, I am fortunate enough to do the rest of these science courses during my next semester exchange programme at Lund University, Sweden, starting in just a few weeks!

As I await my journey to a new place, I would like to reflect on the computer science side of my degree and give a basic review of each unit as well as how important it was for me.
